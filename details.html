Garden of autumn and tenderness

KV4, Hiep Thanh, Nga Bay, Hau Giang

Area

300m2

Description

Like the gentleness of autumn, we designed the garden with quite a lot of autumn trees mixed with light colors, this makes the garden very poetic, suitable for dates. At home, increasing the interest of gardeners, with the poetic beauty of autumn that the garden brings, we can take interactive pictures with the garden every day.

Meaning

Aims to recreate the autumn landscape that heals the human soul through care and healing. Science has proven that when we interact a lot with natural plants, a part of us will become more comfortable with the storms of life and negativity in society. We will know love, not feel lonely anymore, so in addition to reducing environmental pollution, trees can also help heal people's souls gently.
