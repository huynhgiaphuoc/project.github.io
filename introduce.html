Aquatic PLant

Aquatic plants are ornamental plants grown in water that can still live and grow well. When transferred to an aquatic environment, bonsai will not need to be watered regularly, limiting the possibility of plants rotting due to waterlogging, on the other hand, aquatic plants are grown in water, so they are very clean, suitable for making ornamental plants. create excitement for the viewer.
